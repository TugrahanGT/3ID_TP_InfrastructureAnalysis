About Our Company

Our Story

Triple-ID was born as the paths of experienced IAM Consultants crossed and they decided to combine their forces to create the perfect Identity and Access Management consulting company. Each of the initial team members has extensive knowledge on multiple IAM Products including Omada Identity, Oracle Identity Manager, OneIdentity, and SailPoint.

They worked in projects across Europe with customers from diverse industries like finance, automotive, healthcare, and government institutions.

Last but not least, we have also been there, where you as a customer are now, responsible for the integration and maintenance of a product touching almost every system of your company infrastructure.

Our Mission

As Triple-ID, an international company with strong teams in the Netherlands and Turkey, our mission is straightforward: to enable your organization to focus on your core business, while we take care of your IAM journey with measurable, secure, compliant and efficient processes.

On top of that, we have experience working on the vendor side, as an implementation partner as well as customer side. Being able to think like a customer, act like a value-driven partner, and deliver as a vendor, we believe we are a match for any customer that needs help in their IAM journey.
